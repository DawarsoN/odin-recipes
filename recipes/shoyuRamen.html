Shoyu Ramen

Description

Here is the recipe for the delicious Shoyu ramen. The ramen broth is made using soy sauce, giving it its unique flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons fresh grated ginger
  • 2 cups ramen broth (found near the other soup bases/broths at the grocery store)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 6 ounces dry ramen or chukamen noodles (2 3-ounce packages)
  • ramen toppings

Steps

  1. Saut√© garlic and ginger in sesame oil in a large sauce pan until fragrant.
  2. Add ramen broth and soy sauce and simmer for 5-10 mintues.
  3. Strain broth through a fine mesh stainer into a large liquid measuring cup or bowl to remove garlic and ginger bits.
  4. Pour broth back into the sauce pan and discard garlic/ginger pieces. Keep broth at a low simmer until ready to serve.
  5. Prepare ramen or chukamen noodles according to package directions (discard flavor packets if any).
  6. Drain noodles and set aside. Prepare your desired toppings (see above for ideas).
  7. Place broth in individual bowls and divide noodles between the bowls. Top with your favorite protien and vegetable toppings and serve.
